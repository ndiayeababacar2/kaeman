Our Passion

We proud ourselves in designing high quality handcrafted sneakers made with the finest leather and West African traditional fabrics.

Kae-Man makes trendy and elegant footwear for both men and women that appeal to a wide range of tastes. Our products offer unique combinations of colors and patterns handmade by highly talented artisans.

Kae-Man is a social enterprise that empowers local artisans communities and cooperatives through fair trade partnerships.

We are an environmentally conscious company that promotes sustainability in all aspect of our business operations.


Our Mission

We bring the absolute best in style, quality, and authenticity. Our amazing and iconic products are the perfect blend of casual chic and comfort.

Kae-Man shoes are uniquely designed to make you stand out in all occasions.
